Booking Fee Details

What does the Â£5 booking fee cover?

When is the booking fee charged?

  • Only when a family confirms a booking with a sitter

  • Receipt provided immediately via email

Cancellation & Refund Policy

Family Cancellations

  • 24+ hours before: Full refund of booking fee
  • 12-24 hours before: 50% refund of booking fee
  • Less than 12 hours: No refund (sitter compensation)
  • Emergency circumstances: Case-by-case review

Sitter Cancellations

  • Any time before job: Full refund to family
  • Less than 24 hours: Credit voucher for inconvenience
  • No-show situations: Full refund + credit voucher
  • Emergency situations: Full refund, no penalty

Pricing FAQ

Is there a free trial?

Yes! Creating an account and browsing sitters is completely free. You only pay the Â£5 booking fee when you actually need to hire a babysitter.

Can I get a refund if the sitter doesn't show up?

Absolutely. If a sitter fails to show up, you'll receive a full refund of the booking fee plus a credit voucher for the inconvenience.

Do sitters pay anything to use the platform?

No, it's completely free for sitters. They can create profiles, apply for jobs, and message families at no cost. We don't take any commission from their earnings.

Are there any additional charges?

The Â£5 booking fee is the only charge to families. There are no hidden fees, subscription costs, or additional charges.

How do I know what rate to pay my sitter?

Each sitter sets their own hourly rate, which is displayed on their profile. You can also post a job with your budget and let sitters apply.

Can I negotiate rates with sitters?

Yes, you can discuss rates when messaging with sitters. Many are flexible, especially for regular bookings or longer jobs.
