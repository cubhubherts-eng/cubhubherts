Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to join?

It's completely free to create your profile and apply for jobs. We don't charge any membership fees or take commission from your earnings.

How do I get paid?

Families pay you directly in cash or bank transfer at the end of each job. You keep 100% of your hourly rate.

How long does verification take?

Once you submit all your documents (DBS, references, profile), we typically approve applications within 48 hours.

Can I choose my own hours?

Absolutely! You set your availability and only apply for jobs that fit your schedule. You're in complete control.

What if I don't have a DBS check?

We can guide you through the DBS application process. It typically takes 2-4 weeks and costs around ¬£40.

Am I employed by Sitter Squad?

No, you work as an independent childcare provider. You set your rates, choose your jobs, and are paid directly by families.
