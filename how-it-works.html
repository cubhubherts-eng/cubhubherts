Our Business Model

For Families

You pay a small ¬£5 booking fee when you confirm a babysitter. This fee covers our platform costs and ensures commitment from both sides. You pay the babysitter directly for their time at your agreed hourly rate.

Safety & Trust

Safety is our top priority. Here's how we ensure a secure environment for families and babysitters:

Enhanced DBS Checks

All babysitters must have a current enhanced DBS check. We verify the certificate and expiry date.

Reference Verification

We contact references provided by babysitters to verify their experience and suitability for childcare.

Identity Verification

Sitters must provide photo ID and proof of address. Families are verified through email and phone confirmation.

Secure Platform

All data is encrypted and stored securely. Personal details are only shared after confirmed bookings.

Common Questions

What if I need to cancel a booking?

You can cancel up to 24 hours before the booking for a full refund of the booking fee. Last-minute cancellations may not be refundable.

How do I know a babysitter is qualified?

All sitters display verification badges showing their DBS status, qualifications, and verified features. You can see their full profile and reviews before booking.

What happens if there's an emergency?

Sitters have access to emergency contacts and clear procedures. We also provide 24/7 support for urgent situations.

Can I book the same sitter regularly?

Absolutely! Once you find a sitter you trust, you can book them as often as they're available. Many families develop ongoing relationships with their preferred sitters.
